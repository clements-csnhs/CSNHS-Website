What CSNHS is

Computer Science National Honor Society is an organization that recognizes students who display qualities of scholarship, leadership, character, and generosity in relation to computer science. CSNHS strives to promote learning and understanding of computer science, service to the community, and leadership for other students.

What we do

Our members help throughout the community through various activities and events. Students particpate in many activities such as hosting events, running contests, and tutoring students. Members also volunteer throughout the community by running computer science classes at local middle schools as well as helping around the community by providing technology related assistance.

This year's project

Our goal this year is to help expand the impact of the Computer Science National Honor Society by making access to our assistance and services more easily available to all. To do this, we plan to create a web-based service that will allow organizations throughout our community to request assistance so that our members can volunteer to help. In doing so, we hope to be better able to help our community and allow a greater number of people to recieve our help.
